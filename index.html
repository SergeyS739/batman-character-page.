Biography

Batman (born February 19, 1939) is the titular protagonist of the DC Comics franchise. Created by Bob Kane & Bill Finger, first appearance: Detective Comics #27 (1939).

Batman is the alter ego of Bruce Wayne, billionaire owner of Wayne Enterprises. After witnessing his parents' murder, he vowed to rid Gotham of crime. Trained in martial arts, detective work, and strategy, he dons the bat-suit to strike fear.

Allies: Alfred Pennyworth, Commissioner Gordon, Robin. Founding member of the Justice League. Arch-enemy: the Joker, plus Penguin, Riddler, Two-Face.

Portrayed by Michael Keaton, Christian Bale, Robert Pattinson and more.
